Before you begin: data & consent

Card sort study. Researcher: I.F. Jansen, MSc Information Studies, University of Amsterdam (iris.jansen6@student.uva.nl). Supervisor: Dr. L. Stork.

This study investigates how experts categorise automated ontology evaluation methods. The task takes approximately 15 minutes. If you have any questions, contact the researcher at iris.jansen6@student.uva.nl.

How your data is handled

  • Your in-progress sort is stored only in your own browser. Nothing is uploaded automatically.
  • When you click Finish & download, a JSON file is saved locally; you then email it to the researcher.
  • Reopening the same browser may restore earlier work. Use the reset button if you need to start over.
  • Anonymised data is retained on University of Amsterdam secure storage for the thesis project plus one year, and may appear in the MSc thesis and follow-up publications, always anonymised.

Your rights

  • Participation is voluntary. You can stop at any time without giving a reason. You can withdraw your data within two weeks of the session by emailing the researcher.
